Draw With Sound

- By Minjia, Jingrong, Ruiling

Please Follow the Instructions Below!

General Instructions


Take a look at the paper that has 5 labeled sections on it.
We'll play several audio recordings in just a second and ask you to draw something in each section accordingly.

The Audio recordings are different sound effects of drawing (ASMR),
Please, guess and interpret the drawing action played in each audio and imitate the what you heard (whatever you think the drawing action is, just draw the same)
Drawing Instructions
(1) Having a pen or pencil and a piece of paper on the desk in front of you.

(2) Pick up the pen, hold it in your hand with your regular writing gesture and get prepared to draw

(3) Hit the first “play” button on the left, and play the first audio clip. Please listen carefully and draw what you heard within the area labeled as 1. Take your time and make sure to fill the entire section.

(4) Hit the second “play” button, and fill section 2 with what you heard.

(5) Hit the third “play” button, and fill section 3 with what you heard. It doesn't mean that you need to color it all black. Just make sure the pattern you draw takes up the whole area.

(6) Hit the fourth “play” button, and please draw in section 4. Same instructions apply here as above.

(7) Last, hit the fifth “play” button, and please draw in section 5, 6 and 7. Same instructions apply here as above.
